===== DNKK Kano Flight Information Region =====

  * The Kano FIR covers the entirety of [[Nigeria]]'s borders. It is neighbored by [[DGAC|Accra]] FIR in the West, the Niamey FIR in the North, the N'Djamena FIR, the Northeast and [[FCCC|Brazzaville]] in the East and Accra Oceanic in the South.
