===== DNAA Information =====

Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport is an international airport serving Abuja, in the Federal Capital Territory of [[country:nigeria:home|Nigeria]],. It is the main airport serving the Nigerian capital city and was named after Nigeria's first President, Nnamdi Azikiwe. The airport is approximately 20 km (12 mi) southwest of the city centre, and has an international and a domestic terminal that share its single runway.
